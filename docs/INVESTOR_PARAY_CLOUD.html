One-minute pitch

The mall app that replaces Word, Canva and Photoshop.

We already run the hard part on the phone — 20,000 products, shelf labels, and PARAY visual fingerprints: point at a pack, find the article — no reading shelf tickets. Next step: plug PARAY into a small cloud server so every product photo gets pro background removal inside the same app — no re-typing prices in Word or Canva.

Why this exists

Supermarkets lose hours every week on work that should be one tap.

Today: Word, Canva, Photoshop — all manual

Gestium export → re-type every price in Word or Canva → fix images in Photoshop → deliver the image for printing. One price change means starting over.

Our app already fixes 80%

Visio Ai syncs catalog, prints shelf A4 sheets, identifies products by PARAY fingerprints on the aisle (shape, color, packaging learned from photos), shares PNGs — offline-first, built for OASIS MALL scale. No copy-paste from ERP.

What's still painful: manual media work

Cutouts still mean Photoshop time, or a weak phone result — then back to Canva to place the image and re-enter the price by hand.

The unlock

Host PARAY's background-removal AI on one GPU server in Europe. Same button in the app — pro result in ~3 seconds when online.

The key idea — PARAY Cloud

Turn our in-app AI assistant into a paid cloud service clients already need.

Shoot product → tap “PARAY Remove BG” → our VPS runs commercial-grade AI → transparent PNG lands in the catalog → label prints with the correct price. No third-party app. One subscription.

Why a VPS (simple version)
  • One rented server (~€350/mo) serves ~20 shops — cheap vs hours lost in Word, Canva & Photoshop
  • GPU does the heavy math; phones stay fast and offline-capable
  • We control quality, limits, and pricing — not Apple/Google or endless manual layout work
  • EU hosting option for data comfort (photos never sold, only processed)
Why clients will pay
  • Time: cutout + label path drops from ~15 min to ~2 min per article — no Canva re-layout
  • Money: one bill replaces Word/Canva seats, Photoshop time & designer hours
  • Trust: live app at OASIS MALL — not a slide deck
  • Stickiness: catalog + images + print history locked in our ecosystem
